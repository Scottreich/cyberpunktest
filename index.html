What does it mean to be alive? Cyberpunk 2099 explores this. On the corner of 6th and Oak a homeless android begs for enough bit coin to buy himself some code. His software is starting to become ridden with malware. Around the corner an enforcer takes down a terminal tom for peddling code. Not only was this tom selling to a non human, but he doesn't even have certification in any of the languages he writes. Hes sloppy. The world is sloppy.



The Vagarant

The vagarant begs for lines of code. Just enough to get though the day. He needs it to function... literally... He just needs a line of code... the code only a terminal tom can type up.

The Enforcer

Spliced dog/humans. obdient. created in labs by androids. all enforcers are slaves to the system. they are like replicants (blade runner), but they distinctly look like dog-human hybrids. they are pack animals. the entire police force is comprised of these creatures.

Terminal Tom

Who doesn't write code... you need it to survive in this word. Its used as a currency in some indeginous android tribes. But a Terminal tom isn't just some lack luster line buster. Hes the real mccoy. You need a script to prove your travel expense for a trip you never took,..... you need a terminal tom.
